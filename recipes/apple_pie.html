Apple Pie

Description

This is a delicious apple pie that can be made in about 1 hour and baked in 40 minutes. It serves 8 people.

Ingredients

Dough

  • flour, 2 cups
  • salt, 1/2 tsp
  • sugar, 1/2 cup
  • butter, 1/2 cup
  • egg yolk, 2
  • cold water, 2 tsp

Apple Filling

  • cooking apples roughly sliced, 7
  • sugar, 1/2 cup
  • cinnamon, 2 tsp
  • nutmeg, 1 tsp
  • slice of lemon
  • orange juice, 2 tbsp
  • water, 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Cube the butter and combine with flour, salt, sugar, egg yolks, and cold water in a food processor. Process until a rough dough is formed.
  2. Knead dough by hand for 1 minute, then roll out into long rectangle.
  3. Fold into thirds, then wrap with plastic wrap and rest in fridge for 30 minutes.
  4. In a small pot, combine sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon slice, orance juice, and water. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes or until apples are softened. Stir occasionally.
  5. Take dough out of fridge, and roll out into long rectangle again. Fold into thirds.
  6. Separate dough into one big piece and two slightly smaller pieces.
  7. Roll out big piece into roughly 11 inch circle. Place into 9 inch baking pan.
  8. Pour apple mix into pan. Roll out one smaller dough piece into 9 inch circle.
  9. Cut out circle in the middle of rolled-out dough piece. Combine the cut-out circle with the other small dough piece.
  10. Roll out last dough piece into rectangle. Cut into dough strips. Use the strips to form a lattice on the pie.
  11. Put the ring-shaped dough onto the pie, covering the ends of the dough strips.
  12. Brush entire crust with egg wash. Bake at 400 degrees Fahranheit for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
